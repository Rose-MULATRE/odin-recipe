Pineapple and Banana Smoothie

Description

A gorgeous indulgent pineapple smoothie with banana — simple, easy, and delicious!
  • Prep Time:
    5 mins
  • Total Time:
    5 mins
  • Servings:
    1
  • Yield:
    1 smoothie

Ingredients

  • 4 ice cubes
  • ¼ fresh pineapple - peeled, cored and cubed
  • 1 large banana, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup pineapple or apple juice

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Place ice cubes, pineapple, banana, and pineapple juice into the container of a blender.

Step 3

Puree on high until smooth.

Step 4

Enjoy!
